Basic Qualifying Exam

It follows below the topics of my Basic Qualification Exam:
  • Continuous linear mapping between normed linear spaces;
  • Separable normed linear spaces;
  • Banach spaces;
  • Banach-Steinhauss Theorem;
  • Open Mapping Theorem;
  • Reflexive spaces;
  • Initial topologies: Weak and Weak* topologies;
  • Riesz-Fréchet Theorem;
  • Compact operators;
  • Self-adjoin operators;
  • Banach algebras;
  • Gelfand-Mazur Theorem;
  • Spectrum and Spectrum radius;
  • Complex homomorphism;
  • General aspects of Gelfand representation theory;
  • Beurling-Gelfand Theorem;
  • Semisimple commutative Banach algebras;
  • Riesz-Fréchet Theorem;
  • Gelfand-Naimark Theorem.
