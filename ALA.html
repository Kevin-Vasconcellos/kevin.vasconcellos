Professor: César J. Niche

Schedule and classroom

  • Tuesdays: 15:00 - 17:00;
  • Classroom: Block: C, sala: 100-A.

Bibliographic References

Main reference
  • LAX, P. Linear algebra and its applications. Pure and applied mathematics: A Wiley series of texts and monographs. 2007.
Other references
  • OLIVER, P. J; SHAKIBAN, C; SHAKIBAN, C. Applied linear algebra. Upper Saddle Rier, NJ: Prentice Hall, 2006;
  • SERRE, D. Matrices: Theory & Application. L'Ecole Normale Supérieure de Lyon, 2001;
  • STRANG, G. Linear algebra and learning from data. Cambridge Wellesley-Cambridge Press, 2019.

Content

  • Fundamentals of linear algebra: Linear spaces, isomorphisms, basis, dimension, canonical isomorphism, quotient space;
  • Duality: Dual space and Riesz's Lemma;
  • Linear mappings and dimension theorem;
  • Matrices: change of basis and linear mapping;
  • Trace and determinant and their properties;
  • Spectral theory: Eigenvalues and eigenvectors;
  • Euclidean structures: inner dot, orthonormal basis, Gram-Schmidt decomposition, orthogonal projection and isometries;
  • Spectral theory for self-adjoint mappings;
  • Matrix's Inequalities;
  • Decomposition in singular values;
  • Linear normed spaces;
  • Norms of matrices;
  • Spectral radius.
