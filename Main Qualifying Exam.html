Main Qualifying Exam

It follows below the topics of my Ph.D. Qualification Exam:

Homological Algebra

  • Functors and categories;
  • Modules and tensor products;
  • Projective, injective and flat modules;
  • Direct and inverses limites of modules;
  • Homologies functors;
  • Derivative functors;
  • Functors Ext and Tor;
  • Homological dimension.

Commutative Algebra

  • Graded rings;
  • Associated primes and Primary Decomposition Theorem;
  • Completion and Artin-Rees Lemma;
  • Hilber-Samuel Function and ring dimension;
  • Regular sequences;
  • Grade and depth;
  • Koszul complex;
  • Cohen-Macaulay modules and rings;
  • Regular and Gorenstein rings.

Bibliographic References

  • ROTMAN, J. J. An introduction to Homological Algebra. New York: Springer, 2009;
  • ATIYAH, M. Introduction to commutative algebra. CRC Press, 2018;
  • MATSUMURA, H. Commutative ring theory. Cambridge University press, 1989;
  • Bruns, W; Herzog, H. J. Cohen-Macaulay rings. Cambridge University press, 1998.
