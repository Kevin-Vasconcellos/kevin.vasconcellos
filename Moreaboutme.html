More about me

I received my M.Sc. degree in Mathematics at Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ) under advision of professor Hamid Hassanzadeh. I got grade A on all subjects I coursed, obtaining maximum GPA. My Qualification Exam was in Real Analysis and my dissertation was based on the article: A formula for the core of an ideal, by Polini, C. and Ulrich, B.
Currently I am Ph.D. student in Mathematics at UFRJ under supervision of professor Hamid Hassanzadeh and Marc Chardin. I got grade A on all subjects I coursed, obtaining maximum GPA. Although my research field continued being Commutative Algebra, I used the first period of my journey on Ph.D. to improve my knowledge in Algebraic Geometry. My Basic Qualifying Exam was in Functional Analysis, and my Main Qualifying Exam was in Homological Algebra and Commutative Algebra.
